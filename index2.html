Welcome to Freya Rockets an Ethereum Funded initiative!

Freya is a rocket startup that seeks to develop rockets that can place satellites into orbit. Using iterative development and design.
Freya will use computer generated models for designing. 3D PRINTED metal components. Oil rig based launch platform.

Freya is an integrated satellite deployment system. It is primarily funded in ETH(ethereum) using investor pooling and voting process in the design,planning and deployment phase. Construction of components is done through building warehouses and then integrating the components from the warehouses in a single location. The complete rocket will be launched from a sea oil rig. A smart contract will be utilised to fund the project. This contract is ETH based.
