GOLDEN FRIED FRIES
Crispy golden fries made from premium potatoes. RECIPE: Cut potatoes into uniform 8mm sticks, soak in cold water for 30 minutes, pat dry. Package in grease-resistant cardboard containers with dipping sauce compartments for ketchup, mayo, or vinegar. Serve hot for best quality.
